It's known for its simple yet delicious flavor. Pepperoni: A staple in many pizzerias, this pizza is topped with pepperoni slices, mozzarella cheese, and tomato sauce. It's especially popular in the United States. Hawaiian: A controversial but beloved pizza that combines ham and pineapple with cheese and tomato sauce. It originated in Canada but has become a favorite in many countries. Quattro Stagioni: This Italian pizza is divided into four sections, each representing a different season. It typically includes artichokes, ham, mushrooms, and olives, along with cheese and tomato sauce. Pizza Marinara: Another classic from Naples, this pizza is topped with tomato sauce, garlic, oregano, and olive oil. It's known for its straightforward and robust flavors.
